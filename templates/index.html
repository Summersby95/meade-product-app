The MPA is a bespoke internal system for new product development, designed to ensure full product specification information is gathered before the production starts.

The application is currently not open to external vistiors. If you are an employee of Meade Farm Group and have been registered to use the site please Login.

If you are an employee of Meade Farm Group and have not been issued a login please contact a member of the IT team who will assist you in getting an account created.

If you are an external visitor and would like a demo or showcase of the application please email jamessummersby@meadefarm.ie.
